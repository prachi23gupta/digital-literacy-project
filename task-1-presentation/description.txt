I created a digital infographic to explain the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students. I used Canva to design the infographic because it is easy to use and provides a variety of templates and design elements. The infographic includes sections such as the meaning of digital literacy, why it is important in daily life, useful digital tools for students, and basic safe internet practices.
While creating the design, I focused on keeping the content short and easy to understand so that anyone can quickly go through it. I used different colors and simple icons to separate each section and make it visually clear. One challenge I faced was deciding how much information to include, because adding too much text made the design look crowded.
Overall, this task helped me understand how to present information in a clear and structured way using digital tools, and it also improved my ability to design content that is both informative and visually appealing.
